I'm hopeful my GPS will reconnect soon so I can at least get an ETA on Della's route.

AUG 2, 2025

Della the Food Waste Activist

Did you know that the average home throws out almost 200lbs of food into the trash each year? Della — she's such a good girl. Every Saturday Della insists that instead of going for her Saturday walks, she has me drive her around to different neighborhoods so that she can sort through their trash to properly dispose of all food waste. Why, in the past hour we've found enough tossed lasagna scraps and pizza crust to make a family-sized lasagna with "special" bread sticks.

Naturally, Della promises that she's going to use it to start a compost at a local kindergarten to help kids learn about gardening.

And while maybe not as important, Oban wants you to know that the average family of 7 throws out 0.00001% of a tennis ball every 3-6 years. It gets even worse when you find out tennis players often go to the tennis court with a basket of tennis balls because they just have no idea how to properly recycle them. They're the most embarrassed because they always try to tell Oban not to take their used tennis balls, and Oban has to growl sometimes and remind them that it's okay to be so grateful that you don't want him to take their old tennis balls — but not to worry. He will take care of them the proper way.

AUG 6, 2025

Baby Toys

While it's nice to have toys for the baby, I think we should keep in mind how impressionable the pups are as well. Just yesterday I had to talk Oban out of driving a dump truck on a golf course because he believed that he had practiced enough at home with the tonka truck and plastic golf set.

Della I had to go pick up from the court house because the Mattel "My First Court Case" made her think she had passed the bar and was giving really unsound legal counsel to people in the jail house. She did help get one guy out of a speeding ticket. But unfortunately then got him convicted of running an illegal flower shop. Hope the guy doesn't hold a grudge when he gets out in five years.

AUG 9, 2025

The Plate Pusher

It was a good hour and a half before she let me take her plate away. And this is her second lap pushing the bowl around the yard with her nose. The only thing she didn't scarf down was some table etiquette.

AUG 19, 2025

Della Casts a Spell

Was having a drink at the Lava Pit — a hip new tiki bar that a family of raccoons dug out from under a neighbor's porch — when Della turns to me after her 4th sour tennis ball (lemon drop but it's made green using Midori sour) and barks:

"You know what, you're alright. You're a nice guy who helps people and is easy on the paws. I'm gonna do you a favor and use my wiccan magic to summon you a pretty human lady to be your girlfriend."

I try to tell her that's probably not a great idea, and voice concern for her using up so much magic when the next full moon is weeks away. But she ignored me and started to cast right there at the bar. It lasted only a minute, but the melody of the spell was so harmonious that none interrupted her. Not to mention the dazzling magical light energies that paraded around her in all kinds of runes and shapes and colors. As though Della was finishing an order to a waiter, it just ended.

And sure enough, that's when the prettiest lady I've ever seen crawled in through the entrance. It was animal control, and Della had clearly alarmed the upstairs neighbors. We got out a side exit before more AC thugs started to crawl in.

Two lessons learned today: Careful what you wish for. And do not let Della drink and cast — she will make things worse.

The Abandoned Pools

The place is a bunch of underground pools converted into lounges and bars. I really am lucky Oban and Della have these connections, because the car AC was not going to cut it in this heat.

AUG 26, 2025

Della's Campaign Announcement

We're coming up on election season again. And you all know what that means. Della is running for office. Any office — she craves power like the flower craves the sun. It also means she has to once again get out ahead of all the future allegations. So for starters, Della would like to officially address the following:

— Della has never seen your child before and therefore could not have stolen candy from them. Della has never seen any child before in her entire life and all evidence to the contrary is obviously AI generated.

— Della has only ever engaged in peaceful non-violent protest. Any supposed blood on her paws is obviously strawberry preserves from her jarring and pickling hobby.

— Aside from jarring and pickling, Della has no hobbies. Della especially has never nor would ever have a hobby where she, Rutger Hauler, John C. McGinley, F. Murray Abraham, and William McNamara would lure Mr. Ice-T onto a mountainous reservation and attempt to hunt Mr. Ice-T for sport.

— Della has never PERSONALLY embezzled from any of her charities.

That should cover the opening weeks come campaign day.

SEP 10, 2025

Della's Uber Eats Startup

Despite the cool weather I'm sweating like a pig at a BBQ. Della's kept me working 27 straight hours on her newest startup. It's a "tax and fee free Uber Eats" — except the taxes and fees are all bites and portions out of the food. I think I've delivered 23 pizzas so far and every single one is missing 2-3 slices.

Della says with enough investors we can get off the ground and go nationwide. But I'm still not sure where the profit is supposed to come from.

SEP 13, 2025

The Dog Breath Research Project

Dog breath has plagued both man and his best friend since both first broke bread. At last the simplicity of the solution was on our plate all along. The breath isn't the issue — it's the last thing you eat that decides your oral bouquet. So all we have to do is find the best food to be the last thing a dog eats in a meal to preserve our bond and our noses.

Sadly Della explained that my human nose just isn't sophisticated enough, so it's up to Oban and Della to find the perfect last bite. So far every 17 restaurants and 4 grocery stores have failed — or so Oban and Della reassure me.

But they promise they are getting close and that they think pizza looks like a promising next test. I can only hold my breath and hope we discover the perfect food soon so we can monetize it and get back some of the money I've had to spend on "research."

SEP 16, 2025

The Maintenance Report

Just finished maintenance and your pups are good to go. Replaced the subwoofer on Oban for better squirrel deterrent. Roasted the paws so they're good for another 6 months or 300 hikes, whichever comes first.

Gave Della full detail. Started with a wash, then added a new coat of beige followed by a top coat of wax. Overhauled her shoulders so she should be able to claim even more floors and beds. Lastly, filled them both with carrots so they can run till dinner time or at least till the next trip to the fridge for a snack.

SEP 20, 2025

The Trial of Our Generation

Coming to you live from three blocks away from the court house where some are calling it "the trial of our generation." Judge Oban carries the heavy burden of deciding the fate of one man. While prosecutor Della leaves little room for the defendant to breathe.

The crime? Cooking food and then putting leftovers in the fridge instead of on the floor.

Experts say the defendant has only narrowly avoided a guilty verdict due to the evidence mysteriously going missing right before prosecutor Della is about to present it. Or right after Judge Oban asks to examine it in his chamber.

Some are speculating this trial is a mark of government corruption. More open-minded people have offered spectral ghosts and a haunted courtroom as an explanation for these mysteries. At the moment we simply have no way of knowing the truth, and it's possible we may never find this missing evidence.

The Heat Hike

So hot I had to scoop Della up with a dust pan. Oban needed constant watering and popsicles every few steps to survive. But survive we did. All 32 or .2 miles. I'm not so good at judging distance or reading maps, but Della is sure we made it close to Canada.

Mountain People

OCT 28, 2025

The Bank Robbery (Della's Version)

She can hardly be blamed. Della had no idea the bank we stopped at was about to be robbed. And it's only natural Della would put on a ski mask she found to try and blend in with the robbers to avoid getting shot. Naturally, who could blame her for driving the getaway car rather than risk being discovered and treated to all manner of pain by those bank robbing brutes?

And after their getaway plane mysteriously crashed somewhere in South America — there's just no way Della can be blamed for surviving and using those ill-gotten gains to travel back home. Certainly she's just a dog, so how can she be expected to travel efficiently? It's only natural she would take a meandering course through several tourist hot spots, using the stolen money to survive the only way she knew how — which happened to involve a lot of frozen strawberry margaritas and overnights in a series of 5-star hotels.

She was so far from home. She just wanted to feel safe in a penthouse suite. Who could possibly blame her?

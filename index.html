The Tales of
Della & Oban

As Faithfully Reported by Their Walker, Byron

A complete collection of dispatches from the front lines of dog-walking in Marin County — four and a half years of schemes, heists, elections, movie deals, and walks that somehow always ended with the dogs getting what they wanted.

Dedicated to Della Moneybags Rothschild —
muse, schemer, carrot enthusiast, and sometimes bully
when Byron was near a snack.

"If that book ever gets made I don’t see a reason not to
dedicate it to her." — Byron
